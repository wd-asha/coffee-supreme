About this coffee

No. 7, our darkest roast, used to go by the name of Caramel. Its new name is derived from our original shop and roastery at 7 Woodward St, where Coffee Supreme began in 1993, and where it is one of the last remaining menu items from that era. No. 7 is a changing blend of high-grown coffees that are dense enough to handle the very dark roast profile. It’s suitable either on its own for those that like a smoky, low-acid, “old world” cup, or as a seasoning element for adding a deeper, darker taste to one of our other coffees
